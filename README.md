# Humanity Vault AI

Offline-first knowledge preservation platform.

## Mission

Preserve humanity's practical, scientific, cultural, historical, ethical,
and spiritual knowledge for future generations - accessible even when
internet infrastructure is unavailable.

## Core Principles

- Offline First
- Knowledge First
- AI Optional
- Open Source
- Privacy First
- Community Driven
- Free Forever

## Current Status: Alpha 1.0

- 31 articles across all 11 knowledge categories (Survival, Medicine,
  Agriculture, Engineering, Electronics, Construction, History,
  Philosophy, Islam, Local Wisdom, Communication)
- Top 10 priority articles: complete
- Top 25 priority articles: complete
- Knowledge Library, Search, Related Knowledge, and Emergency Mode are
  implemented and integrated

Next milestone: Top 50 expansion and further Alpha hardening.

## Project Layout

- [`humanity_vault_app/`](humanity_vault_app/) - the Flutter
  application (see its [README](humanity_vault_app/README.md) for
  setup and development)
- [`humanity_vault_app/knowledge/`](humanity_vault_app/knowledge/) -
  the knowledge article corpus (Markdown, organized by category)
- [`docs/`](docs/) - planning and process documentation (charter,
