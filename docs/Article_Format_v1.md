# Humanity Vault AI - Article Format Specification v1

## Purpose

This document defines the standard format for all knowledge articles stored in Humanity Vault AI.

The goal is to ensure consistency, verifiability, portability, and future AI compatibility.

---

# Article Structure

Every article must contain:

1. Metadata
2. Summary
3. Main Content
4. Sources
5. Verification Information
6. Related Articles
7. AI Retrieval Metadata

---

# Metadata

Required fields:

title:
category:
subcategory:
author:
created_date:
last_updated:
knowledge_level:
verification_level:
tags:

Example:

title: Water Purification Using Boiling
category: Survival
subcategory: Water
author: Humanity Vault Contributors
created_date: 2026-06-10
last_updated: 2026-06-10
knowledge_level: Level 1
verification_level: Verified Source
tags:

* water
* purification
* survival

---

# Summary

Provide a short summary.

Maximum:

300 words

---

# Main Content

Use Markdown format.

Allowed:

* Headings
* Lists
* Tables
* Images
* References

---

# Sources

Every article should contain source references.

Example:

Sources:

* WHO
* Government Disaster Guide
* Academic Journal

---

# Related Articles

List relevant articles.

Example:

Related:

* Water Storage
* Rainwater Harvesting
* Emergency Hygiene

---

# AI Retrieval Metadata

Purpose:

Improve future semantic search and AI retrieval.

Fields:

retrieval_keywords:
difficulty:
region:
language:

Example:

retrieval_keywords:

* clean water
* boil water
* survival

difficulty:
Beginner

region:
Global

language:
English

---

# File Naming Standard

Use lowercase.

Use underscores.

Example:

water_purification.md

rainwater_harvesting.md

emergency_shelter.md

---

# Image References

Images must be stored separately.

Example:

assets/images/survival/water_purification_01.jpg

Do not embed image files directly inside articles.

---

# Truth Layer Requirement

All factual claims should be traceable to a source.

Articles without sources should be labeled:

Community Source

or

AI Summary
