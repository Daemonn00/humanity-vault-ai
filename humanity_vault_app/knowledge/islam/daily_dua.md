# Daily Morning and Evening Dua

## Summary

A collection of short duas recommended to be recited in the morning and evening for protection, gratitude, and remembrance of Allah.

## Main Content

Reciting morning and evening duas is a Sunnah practice that helps maintain a connection with Allah throughout the day. These duas typically include seeking protection from harm, expressing gratitude, and asking for guidance and well-being for oneself and one's family.

## Benefits

- Provides spiritual protection throughout the day
- Builds a daily habit of remembrance (dhikr)
- Brings peace of mind and gratitude
