# Daily Morning and Evening Dua

## Summary

Morning and evening remembrance (dhikr) is a recommended practice in Islam to seek protection, guidance, and blessings from Allah.

## Recommended Dua

### Seeking Protection

Bismillahil-ladzi la yadurru ma'asmihi syai'un fil ardhi wa la fis sama'i wa huwas sami'ul alim.

### Seeking Well-being

Allahumma inni as'alukal afwa wal afiyah fid dunya wal akhirah.

## Benefits

- Strengthens faith
- Encourages mindfulness of Allah
- Provides spiritual comfort
- Helps establish daily worship habits
