# Basic CPR Overview

## Summary

Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) is an emergency procedure used when a person's breathing or heartbeat has stopped.

## Emergency Steps

### 1. Check Responsiveness

Tap the person and ask loudly if they are okay.

### 2. Call Emergency Services

Request immediate medical assistance.

### 3. Begin Chest Compressions

- Place hands in the center of the chest.
- Push hard and fast.
- Allow full chest recoil.

### 4. Continue Until Help Arrives

Continue CPR until professional help arrives or the person shows signs of recovery.

## Important Notice

This article provides a general overview only.
Formal CPR training is strongly recommended.
