# Water Purification Using Boiling

## Summary

Boiling water is one of the safest methods to reduce harmful microorganisms in untreated water.

## Equipment Needed

- Container
- Heat source
- Water

## Steps

1. Filter visible debris if possible.
2. Bring water to a rolling boil.
3. Maintain boiling for at least 1 minute.
4. Let the water cool before drinking.
