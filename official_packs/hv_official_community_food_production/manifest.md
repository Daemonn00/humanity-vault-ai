This pack covers food production at group and community scale: how to
assess land and plan a diversified growing system for more than one
household, how to build and maintain soil fertility through
composting, how to plan irrigation and understand irrigation-specific
water-safety questions, how to protect crops from pests and disease
without depending on heavy chemical inputs, and how to handle harvest,
storage, and a community food reserve. Each article builds on
Humanity Vault's existing household-scale growing and seed-saving
content rather than repeating it, and cross-links to it throughout.
