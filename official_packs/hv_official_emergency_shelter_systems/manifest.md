---

This pack covers shelter at group, camp, and settlement scale: how to
choose and plan a site for many households rather than one, what
manufactured shelter forms (tarps, tents, and similar) offer compared
to each other, how to manage heat, ventilation, and fire/carbon-
monoxide risk once a shelter is occupied, and how a shared or
transitional settlement is organized and maintained over time. Each
article is written for a general reader planning for or responding to
displacement or disaster, and cross-links to existing Humanity Vault
content on individual wilderness shelter, permanent building, fire-
making, and medical treatment rather than repeating it.
