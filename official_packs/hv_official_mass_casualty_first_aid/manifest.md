This pack contains five official Humanity Vault articles on responding
to injuries beyond single-victim CPR and wound care: triage for
multiple casualties, fracture and sprain stabilization, burn
assessment and field treatment, shock recognition and management, and
hypothermia and heatstroke field response. Each article is written for
someone with basic first aid training, in Humanity Vault's calm,
plain, field-manual register, and traces its claims to the primary
sources listed in its own Sources section.
