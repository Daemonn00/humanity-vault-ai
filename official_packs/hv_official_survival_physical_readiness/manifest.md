---

This pack preserves knowledge for developing physical capability - it
does not and cannot give a reader that capability directly. Strength,
endurance, mobility, balance, and the ability to carry and lift safely
are built only through a person's own repeated physical practice, over
their own time, in their own body. Reading these articles can shorten
and improve that path; it cannot replace it.

The five articles cover progressive physical adaptation, functional
strength and aerobic endurance, mobility and balance, load carrying
and physically-loaded survival tasks, and recovery and injury
prevention. They are written for a general reader with no assumed
gym access, equipment, or prior fitness training, and are meant to
complement - not replace - this project's existing emergency medical
content.
