---

This pack covers water treatment for a group or community sharing a
water source, distinct from the single-household techniques already
covered elsewhere in Humanity Vault (boiling, household storage, and
single-family well construction). Each article explains a stage of
the same underlying chain - from assessing how much water is needed
and protecting its source, through pre-treatment, filtration, and
disinfection, to safe storage, distribution, and ongoing monitoring -
so a reader can understand how these stages fit together as one
system, not as a set of disconnected techniques. No single method in
this pack removes every category of contaminant on its own; the
articles are written to be used together, and cross-link to
Humanity Vault's existing household-scale water content rather than
repeating it.
