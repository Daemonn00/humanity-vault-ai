# Research Plan Template

Fill this in after a topic is accepted (`TOPIC_SELECTION_TEMPLATE.md`)
and before writing begins. Its purpose is to plan what needs to be
verified, per article, so research time is spent deliberately rather
than writing first and hoping a source turns up afterward. This
directly feeds `SOURCE_COLLECTION_TEMPLATE.md` (what was actually
found) and, later, `editorial_review_template/`'s Claim-to-Source Map
(what was actually verified).
