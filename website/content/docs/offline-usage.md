# Offline Usage

Humanity Vault AI is built offline-first: once installed, the app
needs no internet connection for anything in its core library.

## What works fully offline

- Browsing categories and articles.
- Searching the library.
- Emergency Mode and terrain-specific topic ordering.
- Ask the Vault (retrieval over your installed articles and packs).
- Installed Knowledge Packs, once they're on the device.

## What needs a network connection

- **Vault Hub's catalog** &mdash; browsing and downloading new
  Official Knowledge Packs requires a connection. If you're offline,
  Vault Hub shows the last catalog it successfully loaded, clearly
  marked as offline, rather than an empty screen.
- **This website** &mdash; it's an online-only companion to the app,
  not something the app depends on.

Once a pack is downloaded and installed, it behaves exactly like the
app's built-in content: fully available with no connection at all.
