Why Humanity Vault exists

Humanity Vault AI is a free, offline-first knowledge library for survival, recovery, and rebuilding. The app works with zero network dependency: no required account, no required internet connection, and no cost, ever.

Optional Knowledge Packs extend the app's built-in library with additional, source-verified articles. Every official pack listed on this site is published the same way the app itself installs packs — as a plain, inspectable ZIP file.
